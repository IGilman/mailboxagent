Our Story

John Smith

Owner

Hi, my name is John and I'm based in Charlotte, NC. I started Mailbox Masters to help homeowners enhance their curb appeal with beautiful, functional mailboxes.

After seeing too many poorly installed mailboxes that quickly deteriorated, I knew there was a better way. Drawing on my background in construction and design, I created a company dedicated to quality craftsmanship and excellent customer service.

I love turning this everyday necessity into a standout feature for your home that will last for years to come.
